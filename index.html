Dictionary Definition

Before I begin to define MY FAMILY, let's take a look at what the dictionary says a family is.

According to Merriam-Webster, a family is

the basic unit in society traditionally consisting of two parents rearing their children.

I get it. I'm not mad at MW for defining it that way. For MANY years, my family kind of fit that definition perfectly. But honestly, that was only on the surface.
